Overview

One Tap Chef was the second mobile game I developed and my first commercialised product. It was made in Unity 5.4. It is a very simple mobile game where the player has to click the food on the grill at the correct time. If food is cooked perfectly the player gains points. If the food is burned or undercooked then the player loses points.
I experimented with audio cues whilst developing this game, they help the player better understand the rules of the game.


Skills I learned from this project:

  • Improved C# knowledge

  • Improved mobile game development knowledge

  • Monitisation of a mobile game

  • Unity mobile development

  • Integration of a Facebook friends highscore system
